@TheTweetserve

@TheTweetserve will maybe retweet you if:

  • you follow it, and
  • you've mentioned it recently
If it retweets you, then it will follow you back.

It picks who to retweet from the eligible participants at random. That's what makes it special.

If you've got a problem with it or an idea about how to make it better, please file an issue.
